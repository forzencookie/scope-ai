# Förmåner (Employee Benefits) — Swedish Tax Rules 2026

Source: Skatteverket, Inkomstskattelagen (IL), Socialavgiftslagen

---

## Skattefria förmåner (Tax-Free Benefits)

These benefits carry no income tax or employer social contributions for the employee.

### Friskvårdsbidrag
- **Limit:** 5 000 kr/year per employee
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Tax-free up to 5 000 kr. Above limit → taxable as salary.
- **Applies to:** All company types (AB, EF, HB/KB)

### Personalvård (Fika, Frukt, Kaffe)
- **Limit:** None — unlimited if available to all employees at the workplace
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Coffee, fruit, snacks at the office are fully tax-free. Must be available to all employees, not just a few.
- **VAT:** 25% moms on the purchase is deductible as ingående moms

### Julgåva (Christmas Gift)
- **Limit:** 550 kr/employee (incl. VAT)
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Tax-free up to 550 kr. Above limit → entire amount becomes taxable (not just the excess).
- **Cannot be:** Cash or gift cards redeemable for cash

### Jubileumsgåva (Milestone Gift)
- **Limit:** 1 650 kr/employee (incl. VAT)
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Tax-free when company celebrates 25, 50, 75-year anniversary or employee has worked 25 or 50 years

### Minnesgåva (Long-Service/Farewell Gift)
- **Limit:** 15 000 kr/employee (incl. VAT)
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Tax-free on retirement or after minimum 6 years of service. Only one minnesgåva per employment.

### Cykelförmån (Bicycle Benefit)
- **Limit:** 3 000 kr/year (employer contribution)
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Employer can contribute up to 3 000 kr/year toward employee bicycle. Tax-free if bicycle used primarily for commuting.

### Arbetskläder (Work Clothes)
- **Limit:** None if clearly work-specific
- **Account:** 5810 Arbetskläder
- **Rule:** Tax-free when branded/unsuitable for private use. Regular clothes = taxable.

### Företagshälsovård (Occupational Health)
- **Limit:** Reasonable costs
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Fully tax-free. Includes medical check-ups, ergonomic assessments, vaccinations.

### Utbildning (Education/Training)
- **Limit:** None for work-relevant training
- **Account:** 7620 Utbildning
- **Rule:** Tax-free when the training benefits the employer's business. Language courses, professional certifications = tax-free.

### Reseförsäkring (Travel Insurance)
- **Limit:** None for business travel coverage
- **Account:** 6310 Försäkringspremier
- **Rule:** Tax-free when covering business travel.

## Milersättning (Mileage Allowance)

### Privat bil i tjänsten (Private Car for Business Use)
- **Rate 2026:** 25 kr/km (from 2025, increased from 18.50 kr/km)
- **Tax-free up to:** 25 kr/km — above this is taxable salary
- **Account:** 7331 Bilersättningar
- **How to book:** Debit 7331, Credit 1930 (bank)
- **Documentation:** Mileage log required (from/to, purpose, km)

### Tjänstebil — Drivmedel (Company Car Fuel)
- **Rule:** If employer pays fuel for private driving → taxable benefit (förmånsvärde)
- **Drivmedel förmånsvärde multiplier 2026:** 1.2× actual fuel cost (employee taxed on this)
- **Account for employer cost:** 5611 Drivmedel för tjänstebilar

## Skattepliktiga förmåner (Taxable Benefits)

These benefits are taxed as salary. The employer must add förmånsvärdet to the employee's gross salary for withholding tax and AGI.

### Tjänstebil (Company Car)
- **Account:** 5610 Personbilar (cost), 7380 Förmåner till anställda (förmånsvärde)
- **Förmånsvärde:** Calculated by Skatteverket based on car list price
  - Base: 29% of car list price per year
  - Environmental discount: Electric/hybrid cars get reduction (up to 40% discount)
  - Added to employee salary monthly for tax withholding
- **Documentation:** Skatteverket calculation sheet required

### Bostad (Housing Benefit)
- **Account:** 7380 Förmåner till anställda
- **Förmånsvärde:** Market rental value of the accommodation

### Parkering (Parking)
- **Account:** 7380 Förmåner till anställda
- **Förmånsvärde:** Market value of the parking space per month

### Telefon (Mobile Phone)
- **Since 2018:** Tax-free if employer needs employee to be reachable
- **Account:** 6212 Telefon
- **Rule:** Fully tax-free for a single mobile subscription

### Lunch (Subsidized Lunch)
- **Förmånsvärde 2026:** 55 kr/meal (standardbelopp per Skatteverket)
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** If employer subsidizes lunch below market rate, the difference is taxable

## Bruttolöneavdrag (Salary Sacrifice)

Employee trades gross salary for a benefit — reduces taxable income.

### Tjänstepension (Occupational Pension)
- **Account:** 7530 Pensionskostnader
- **Tax rule:** Employer contributions to pension are deductible. Employee portion via salary sacrifice reduces taxable income.
- **Limit:** 35% of salary or 10 PBB (prisbasbelopp), whichever is lower

### Sjukvårdsförsäkring (Private Health Insurance)
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Employer-paid health insurance is a taxable benefit (förmån). Employee salary sacrifice reduces gross for tax.

### Cykelförmån via bruttolöneavdrag
- **Account:** 7690 Personalvård
- **Rule:** Employee sacrifices gross salary for a bicycle. Reduces taxable income.

## Key Rules to Remember

1. **Julgåva threshold is absolute** — if you spend 600 kr on a gift, the full 600 kr is taxable, not just the 50 kr above the limit.
2. **Friskvård must be simple** — gym membership is fine, spa/massage packages are not.
3. **All employees must have equal access** to personalvård (fika etc.) — selective benefits are taxable.
4. **Tjänstebil förmånsvärde** is added to gross salary before tax withholding — it does not flow through the company's cost accounts directly.
5. **Mileage logs** are mandatory for milersättning — Skatteverket can deny the deduction without one.
